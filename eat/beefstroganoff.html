·Beef Stroganoff·

6 lbs. steak cubes
3 cans beef consommé
3 large onions chopped
2 cans of mushrooms
1 can of tomato paste
1/2 lb. butter
16 oz. sour cream
1/2 cup sherry

Brown meat with onions in butter. Add consommé, tomato paste and mushrooms. Simmer for 2 hours. Thicken with flour, add sherry and sour cream.
