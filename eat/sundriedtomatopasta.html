·Sundried Tomato Pasta·

Pasta
Chicken, or ham, or bacon (or a combination of the three)
Sundried Tomatoes
1 Onion

Cook the pasta as directed on the box. Cook the chicken in a frying pan. In another frying pan, pour 2 tblsp of oil (either olive oil or the one from the Sundried Tomatoes). Cut the cooked chicken, the onion, and the Sundried Tomatoes in little pieces and add to the oil. Let it cook until the onion is tender. Serve on top of cooked pasta.
