·Potato Salad·

5 lbs. potatoes, cooked and cubed
1 cup sliced green onions
1/2 cup Italian dressing
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup prepared mustard
3 teaspoons salt
2 cups sliced celery
4 hard cooked eggs, chopped
1/2 cup sweet pickle relish

Combine potatoes, onion and Italian dressing; toss gently to coat.
Cover and cill 1 hour. Combine mayonnaise, mustard and salt; pour over potato mixture.
Toss gently to coat. Add celery, eggs and relish; toss. Chill several hours.
Garnish with parsley and green pepper rings.
