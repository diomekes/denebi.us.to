·Banana Bread·

3 ripe bananas
1 stick of butter
3/4 cup of sugar
2 eggs
2 cups of flour
1/2 cup of nuts (optional)

Turn the oven on 325F. Mix the butter and the sugar together. Peel and cut the bananas in small pieces before mashing them with the sugar and butter mix. Add the eggs. Add the flour and the nuts to the mix and blend well. Put it in a pan to go into the oven. Let cook for 60 minutes (it doesnt always take this long) or when the bread is lightly brown.
