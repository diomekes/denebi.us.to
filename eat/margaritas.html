·Margaritas·

1/2 cup of squeezed limones
3/4 cup of controy (aka orange liquor)
1 1/2 – 2 cups of tequila
1/4 cup of jarabe natural (or simple syrup)

Blend. Add ice to fill blender.
Makes enough for 6 large margaritas.

To salt the cups, pour some jarabe natural on a plate. On a second plate pour some margarita salt. Place cup first in jarabe, then the salt.

Enjoy!
