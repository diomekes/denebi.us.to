·Mexican Red Rice·

2 tomatoes
1/2 onion
1 tsp of salt
1 tblsp of chicken bouillon powder
1 1/4 cup of rice
2-3 tblsps of oil
1 garlic clove

Combine the tomatoes, the onion, the salt, and chicken bouillon in a blender.
Add water to the blend to make it 2 1/2 cups.
Meanwhile, put the oil in a pan and heat it up. Place the garlic clove with the rice in the pan.
Once the rice is a little browned add the blend to the rice and cover on low heat. It is ready once the liquid is all gone.
