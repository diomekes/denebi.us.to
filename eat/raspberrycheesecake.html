·Raspberry Cheesecake·

Filling:
2 8 oz of vegan cream cheese
1 tsp of vanilla
1 raspberry soy yogurt
1/3 cup of sugar
Crust:
1 1/2 cup of granola
1/4 cup of butter
1 tbs of maple syrup
1 tbs of oat flour
Topping:
Fresh raspberries

Preheat the oven at 350 F
For the filling, mix the yogurt with the cream cheese in a bowl. Then add the sugar and the vanilla to it.
For the crust, in another bowl mix all the dry ingredients together. Then add the butter and the maple syrup to the mix. Mix it until the granola stick together to make it look like a paste. Place the paste in a cooking pan with the filling. Let it cook for about 25 minutes. Once it has cooled down place it in the fridge for about 3 hours. Before serving, add the fresh raspeberries to the cake.
