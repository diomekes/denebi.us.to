·Baked Ziti with sausage·

1 lb of Ziti pasta
1 lb of sausage
2 cans of cubed tomatoes
2 tsp of oregano
2 tsp of basil
1 garlic clove
1 bag of Parmesan cheese

Preheat oven at 350. Cooked the pasta according to the package direction. Meanwhile, cooked the sausage with the garlic in a frying pan. Once the sausage is cooked add the diced tomatoes and the seasoning; let simmer for 5 minutes or until the pasta is done. Once the pasta is cooked take a bowl that can go in the oven and layer the pasta and the sauce, alternating them. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Cook for 30 minutes and then broil for 15 minutes.
