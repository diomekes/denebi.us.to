·Mashed potatoes·

6 Potatoes
1 stick of butter
salt and pepper
1/4 cup of milk

Peel the potatoes, cut them into five parts each and boil them in water. When you can stick a fork through them it means they are ready. Drain off the water. Then mash them up with a meat masher, add butter, salt and pepper, milk and beat them with an electric beater.
