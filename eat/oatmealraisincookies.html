·Oatmeal Raisin Cookies·

1 stick of butter
1 cup of brown sugar
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups of flour
1 tsp of cinnamon
3 cups of Quaker Oats
1 cup raisins

Heat the oven to 350F. Beat the butter and the sugar together until creamy. Add the eggs and beat well. Add the flour and the cinnamon and beat well. Stir in the oat and the raisins and mix well. On a cooking sheet drop rounded tblsps. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until golden brown.
