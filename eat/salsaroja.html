·Salsa Roja·

5 red tomatoes
3 chilies
1/2 small onion
1 tsp of chicken bouillon/seasoning
1/2 cup of water
handful of chopped cilantro
a pinch – 1 tsp of salt

Roast the plum tomatoes and chilies until tomatoes are soft and everything is blackened. Place all ingredients in blender – blend slightly – too much will make it too foamy.
