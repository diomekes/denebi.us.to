·Homemade Marinara Sauce·

2 tsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic
2-3 Roma tomatoes or 2 handfuls of cherry tomatoes
1/4 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp salt
black pepper
1 bell pepper (optional)

Preheat a saucepan over med-low. Add oil, garlic, and tomatoes. Saute until the tomatoes are sweating (you can see drops forming on them) and then crush them with a potato masher or a fork. I keep the peel in the sauce. Season the sauce and let it cook for 5 more minutes.

I like to mix it with white sauce when I make pizza.
