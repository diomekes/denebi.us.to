·Brown Rice·

1.5 cups rice
1/2 stick of butter
1/2 onion
2 cups beef consommé
Enough water to make 3 cups with the consommé

Chop onion and sauteé with half of a stick of butter until onion is slightly brown. Add rice and mix, stir around until rice is golden. Place all ingredients in a casserole dish, adding the beef consommé and water (3 cups total). Place dish in oven at 350º and let cook for half an hour to 45 minutes.
