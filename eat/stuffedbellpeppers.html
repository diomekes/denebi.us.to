·Stuffed Bell Peppers·

2 Italian turkey sausages
a couple of tomatoes
2 shallots
1/4 tsp of basil
1/4 tsp of oregano
a dash of paprika and crushed red peppers
3 bell peppers
Hummus or cheese

Preheat the oven at 350. Cook the sausages in a pan with olive oil. Meanwhile, cut the tomatoes and the shallots and place in a mixing bowl. Once the sausages are cooked add them to the mixing bowl and season. Cut the bell peppers in half, take the seeds out and wash the peppers. Oil a pan that can go in the oven, place the peppers in the pan and stuff them. I cover mine with hummus and my boyfriend puts cheese on his…so do whatever suits u…I also sprinkle some paprika over the hummus. Cook for 15-20 min and serve with rice.
