·Tinga·

2 halves of chicken
1 tblsp of chicken bouillon
Salt and Pepper
2 1/4 onions
2 tblsp of oil
2 tomatoes
1 chipotle pepper and some of its juice
1 Garlic clove

Put the chicken to boil with the chicken bouillon, salt, and pepper. Once it is cooked let it cool off for a few minutes. Then shred the chicken in tiny pieces. Slice two onions into circles and sautee them in a frying pan with the two tblsp of oil. While the onions are cooking put the tomatoes, chipotle and its juice, the garlic clove, and 1/4 of an onion in the blender and blend it all together. Pour the mix onto the onions in the frying pan and let it cook for about 10 minutes. Finally, add the shredded chicken to the pan and let it simmer for about an hour or so.
