·Vegan Mac and Cheese·

1 1/2 cups of plain soy milk
1 cup of water
1/3 cup of tamari or soy sauce
1 1/2 cup of nutritional yeast
1 tablespoon paprika
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon salt
1/4 of a block of firm (not silken) tofu
1 cup of canola or vegetable oil
1 1/2 lbs of macaroni pasta
1 dollop of mustard (optional)

Preheat oven to 350ºF.
Cook pasta following the directions on the box.
Blend all the ingredients, except the pasta, together.
After draining the pasta, place it in a baking pan and pour the cheese sauce over it.
Cook in oven for about 15 minutes or until golden brown.
