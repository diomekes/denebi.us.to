·Cole Slaw·

1 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons vinegar base BBQ sauce
2 to 3 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon ketchup
1 tablespoon cider vinegar
1 teaspoon salt or more
1 medium head cabbage, grated

Shake together the mayonnaise, barbecue sauce, sugar, ketchup, vinegar, and salt until well blended.
Place the cabbage in a large bowl and pour the dressing over it, then toss together. Chill for at least 1 hour.
