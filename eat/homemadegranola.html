·My homemade granola·

1 cup of Oat flakes
1 tbs of canola oil
1-2 tbs of sunflower seeds
1 tbs of flax seeds
Almonds, dried apples, raisins (I eyeball it)

Preheat oven to 375 F
Mix the oat flakes, the canola oil, and the sunflower seeds.
Place the mix in a baking dish. Cook for 5-10 minutes.
Meanwhile, place the rest of the ingredients in the container you want to use.
Remove the baking dish from the oven, let it cool and then place it in the container.
Now enjoy it with milk or yogurt.
