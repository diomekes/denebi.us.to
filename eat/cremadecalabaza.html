·Crema de Calabaza·

5 medium zucchini
1/4 onion
1tsp of salt
1/2 stick of butter
1/4 to 1/2 cup of half & half cream
1 liter to 1 1/2 liters of milk

Slice the zucchinis and the onion, then boil them in water until they are soft. Drain them and puree them in the blender along with the salt. In a big pot, melt 1/2 stick of butter and add the puree to it and let it sauté for a few minutes (while stirring). Then add the cream and stir it until it is mixed well. Finally, add the milk as you stir. Let it heat up to just before it starts boiling (Don't let it boil).
